# Product

**SuinoGestor** is a swine farm management application targeting Brazilian pig producers. It transforms zootechnical and biological data into strategic business decisions, helping producers transition from informal management to professional, data-driven operations.

## Core Domain

The product operates in the Brazilian swine production sector (suinocultura), where Brazil is the 4th largest global producer. Key domain concepts:

- **Matrizes** (sows): breeding females tracked individually across their reproductive lifecycle
- **Lotes** (batches): groups of animals managed together through production phases
- **Sistema de Lotes**: batch management system using "all in, all out" sanitary protocols
- **Vazio Sanitário**: mandatory rest/disinfection period between batches (typically 7 days)
- **IDE** (Intervalo Desmame-Cobertura): weaning-to-coverage interval, key reproductive KPI

## Main Modules

1. **Gestão Reprodutiva** – sow lifecycle tracking, coverage calendars, discard alerts
2. **Planejamento de Lotes** – batch scheduling, room calculators, production targets
3. **Nutrição e Hidratação** – feed cost control, water monitoring, waste tracking
4. **Sanidade e Bem-Estar** – disease alerts, vaccination schedules, MMA syndrome detection
5. **Econômico e Mercado** – corn/swine price ratio analysis, cost calculators
6. **Ambiental** – waste volume management and biofertilizer dosage

## Target Users

Small to large Brazilian pig farms, both integrated (integração contratual) and independent (mercado spot) producers.

## Language

All domain content, UI text, and documentation is in **Brazilian Portuguese**.
